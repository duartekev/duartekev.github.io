Types of Renewable Energy

Solar energy: is what is obtained from radiation from the sun, and can be of two types;

Photovoltaics, when sunlight is used and transformed into electricity through the use of photovoltaic solar panels, and

Thermal, by taking advantage of the sun's heat through thermal collectors that absorb and concentrate said heat.

Wind energy: It is obtained by transforming the kinetic energy of the wind into electricity. It can be of two types, depending on where the wind turbines are located.

Onshore wind power takes advantage of currents of air produced on land, and

Offshore wind, is nourished by the force of the wind that is produced on the high seas.

Geothermal energy: This energy uses the thermal energy available from the Earth's interior. Heat is extracted from geothermal deposits through wells or other means.
